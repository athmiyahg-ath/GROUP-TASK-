## Introduction
Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think and learn. It has become a pivotal part of modern technology, influencing areas from healthcare to finance.

## History of AI
- 1950s: Concept of AI introduced.
- 1960s–70s: Early AI programs developed.
- 1980s: Rise of expert systems.
- 2000s–Present: Machine learning and deep learning breakthroughs.

## AI Techniques
### Machine Learning
Techniques that allow systems to learn from data and improve performance over time.

### Deep Learning
Subset of machine learning involving neural networks with multiple layers.

### Natural Language Processing
AI methods that enable machines to understand and process human language.

## Applications of AI
- Healthcare: Diagnosis and treatment recommendations
- Finance: Fraud detection and algorithmic trading
- Transportation: Autonomous vehicles
- Robotics: Intelligent machines performing complex tasks

## Conclusion
Artificial Intelligence continues to evolve, with significant impacts on industry, research, and daily life. Understanding its concepts and applications is crucial for future technological advancements.
